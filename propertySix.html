Property Six


Opportunity to acquire a traditional period building that has recently been converted into 9 luxury apartments, with freehold, in the highly desirable area of Meads Village, Eastbourne. The apartments are spread over three floors and have been finished to a high specification. Each apartment is unique and has 2-bedrooms and 2-bathrooms (a master bathroom and an en-suite), gas central heating and access to the communal gardens, with 4 apartments having their own private garden. There are seven parking spaces in front of the building available to purchase and the development has an ICW warranty.The total size of the property is 8,341 sqft.

The property briefly comprises; reception porch leading to hallway. The kitchen is fitted with a range of cupboards and drawers both at base and eye level, whilst allowing room for modern appliances with plenty of work surfaces for food preparation. A feature archway opens onto the formal dining room creating a social environment for families to cook and dine together. The spacious dual aspect sitting room has a fireplace.On the first floor are four good size bedrooms and a family bathroom.


The property is approached via a shared resident's driveway which leads to the garage block with a single garage providing parking and storage. There is a communal garden to the front of the property, along with a separate amenity garden for airing clothes etc. There is a secluded private garden detached from the house, situated approximately 25m from the front door. The garden is predominantly laid to lawn with a garden shed and is a blank canvas to landscape to a desired taste. The elevated position takes full advantage of the wonderful countryside and river views.


The property occupies a quiet corner of a small modern cul-de-sac scheme and benefits from an unusually large private garden and terrace in which all rear rooms overlook. A large central dual aspect reception hall has a sweeping feature staircase with concealed storage cupboard below and attractive polished wood flooring which continues through the ground floor level. The sitting room has a attractive contemporary stone fireplace and is a lovely bright space with full-length windows to two sides and access to the terrace area and garden.
