The Fulford Road Development is located on Fulford Road. A short walk from the River Ouse and the stunning City Centre of York. The location is perfect for both long term residents and short term holiday makers/business visitors as it has a wealth of local facilities as well as the city cent on its door step. Notable local landmarks are the river ouse walk, Clifford's Tower and the Castle Museum, all a short walk away.


The development was formerly two large turn of the century townhouses, number 71 and number 73 Fulford Road. Once grand residences the properties were more recently operated as boutique bed and breakfasts before the current development took place. The new development has extensively renovated both properties and now offers a unique range of properties.


Number 71 has been converted into 4 apartments, a spacious one bedroomed apartment benefiting from a large bay window to the front of the property on the ground floor, a two bedroomed apartment on the first floor, a two bedroomed duplex to the rear over the ground and first floors and the second and third floors have been designed into a stunning 3 bedrooms duplex penthouse with loft style living space and balcony


Number 73 has retained the prestigious town house which offers 7 large ensuite bedrooms arrange over the ground and first floors off a grand entrance hall and staircase and a living/entertaining suite on the top floor that takes advantage of the roof heigh and original features to create a one of a kind living/dining/kitchen space. This property would make a one of a kind statement city centre residence or a highly profitable shot term let opportunity. To the rear of 73 the development is completed with two further apartments both with their own access.
