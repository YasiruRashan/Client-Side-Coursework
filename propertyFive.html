Property Five


The property is arranged over the first and second floors of this mid terraced Victorian property and offers light in abundance due to its East to West orientation. The accommodation is laid out at just over 1500 sq ft, entered via its own entrance on the raised ground floor. The stairs lead you to the first floor landing taking you to a beautiful main reception which features a period fireplace and sash windows, kitchen/diner with direct access to its own section of rear garden and refitted shower room. The upper floor has two further double bedrooms with over 100sqft worth of extra storage space in the loft.
